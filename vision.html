Vision & Mission

Why we still draw it by hand first.

Positron was founded on a simple premise: a practice can stay personal and still be trusted with the largest, most demanding sites. Everything below is the working version of that premise.

FOUNDED 1993

A practice built by three architects, on purpose.

Ar. S.N. Iyer, Ar. Rakesh S. Chaumal and Ar. N.C. Daftary founded Positron and split the practice deliberately rather than competing for the same work — one division for industrial clients, one for urban and residential, and a third role dedicated entirely to keeping projects moving through statutory authorities. That division of labour is still the shape of the firm today.

TWO STUDIOS

Thane for industry. Ghatkopar for the city.

The Industrial Projects Division works out of Sambhav IT Park in Wagle Industrial Area, Thane — close to the plants, parks and MIDC plots it serves. The Urban Projects Division sits in Kailash Plaza, Ghatkopar, closer to the residential and institutional clients of central Mumbai.

Mission statement

Four commitments, unchanged since the practice was founded.

01

Stay small enough to give personal attention.

To remain small enough to give personal attention to a select group of repeat clients, yet large enough to handle their largest and most demanding projects.

02

Foster excellence and integrity together.

To foster an environment where professional excellence and integrity is perennially pursued — neither traded off against the other.

03

Keep updating skills, technology and knowledge.

Constant updating of skills, technology and knowledge to remain abreast with changing times and needs.

04

Make every engagement mutually satisfying.

To ensure that all professional engagements are mutually satisfying — for the client, and for the people doing the work.
